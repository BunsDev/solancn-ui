## Customizing Your Flipping Words

You can easily replace the text being flipped with your own words by updating the `phrases` array:

```tsx
const phrases = [
  "AWESOME",
  "BEAUTIFUL",
  "STUNNING",
  "COOL",
  "ELEGANT",
  "AMAZING",
  "VIBRANT",
  "DYNAMIC",
];
